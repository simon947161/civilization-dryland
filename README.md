# Civilization Dryland

Civilization Dryland is a long-term research repository about dryland regions, climate adaptation and the future of human settlement.

Drylands are not empty spaces. They are memory systems, survival systems and civilization frontiers. Many of humanity's oldest lessons about water, land, shelter, movement, agriculture and governance come from dry environments.

This repository explores how those lessons can help us think about the future.

## Research direction

This repository may include notes and studies on:

- Dryland civilization history
- Climate adaptation knowledge
- Water and settlement patterns
- Ecological transition
- Regional resilience
- Desert-edge development
- Long-term environmental observation
- Civilization-scale sustainability thinking

## Connection to ClimateOS

ClimateOS is not only a technical system. It also needs cultural memory, historical wisdom and regional knowledge.

Dryland regions teach one essential lesson: survival depends on understanding limits. A future operating system for climate and civilization should learn from places where limits have always been visible.
